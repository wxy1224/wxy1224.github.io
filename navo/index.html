Shopping … made however YOU wanted it to be


Ever had that pain when you finally found your dream outfit, only to get stuck at that huge fitting room or checkout line? Never again! With Navo, you know in real time how long each line is, so you can just

  • Check out on your cellphone and have your items delivered or pick up in store

  • Let Navo do the route planning which avoids crowded hours

Worried about Your Privacy or Battery Life?


We value your privacy, and that’s why even when you’re using the navigation function, your location remains anonymous. The only people who will know where you are through Navo are your friends whom you are sharing locations with, when you share it. We will ask for your permission every single time we position your cellphone, and you can always turn it off in your setting.

To protect your battery life, we run all the algorithms locally on your device, so there won’t be constant communication between your phone and our server.
